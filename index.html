About Us

Western Motorcoach is a full-service charter bus, shuttles, and tours company specializing in group travel for over 15 years. Based in Houston, we provide luxury charter bus rentals, private charter bus and motorcoach services, school transportation services, wedding and conventions transportation, corporate shuttles, casino shuttles, custom tours for private groups and exciting bus trip to many popular destinations.
